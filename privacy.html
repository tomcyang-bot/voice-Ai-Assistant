Privacy Policy

Provided by Simply Project Consulting
Last Updated: March 30, 2026

Our commitment: Simply Project AI is built with a "Privacy by Design" approach. We only request the minimum permissions needed to provide our service, and we never sell your data.

1. Introduction

Simply Project AI (provided by Simply Project Consulting) is a productivity tool designed for the Australian building and consultancy industry. We value your privacy and apply a "Privacy by Design" approach to all our features.

2. Information We Collect and Use

  • Google Calendar Datacalendar.events only
    We request access solely to create, edit, or view calendar events based on your voice or text commands. We do not store your full calendar history on our servers.
  • Email Data (Mailto)Zero Gmail API
    We do NOT request access to read or send your emails via the Gmail API. All email drafts are generated locally on your device using mailto: links — your email content never leaves your own mail client.
  • Voice Data
    Voice inputs are processed in real-time to extract intent. We do not store, sell, or use your voice data for advertising purposes.
  • Authentication Tokens
    Google OAuth tokens are stored locally in your browser's localStorage and are never sent to our servers. They are cleared on logout.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Minimal storage: We do not store your calendar events, email content, or voice recordings on our servers.
  • Encrypted infrastructure: Any essential metadata is stored on encrypted, secure servers.
  • No third-party sharing: We do not share your personal or project data with any third parties, except as required to provide the service (e.g., Google API, Anthropic Claude API, OpenAI Whisper API).

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google APIs — OAuth, Calendar. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI intent processing. Not used for model training. Governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI Whisper API — Voice transcription. Not stored long-term. Governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — Hosting infrastructure. Governed by Vercel's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

  • You can revoke our access to your Google account at any time via your Google Security Settings.
  • Once access is revoked, we can no longer access any of your data.
  • We do not retain user data after access is revoked.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated within the application. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Simply Project AI（由 Simply Project Consulting 提供）是专为澳洲建筑及咨询行业设计的生产力工具。我们极其重视您的隐私，并对所有功能采用"隐私原生"设计。

2. 我们收集和使用信息的方式

  • Google 日历数据仅 calendar.events
    我们申请此权限，仅用于根据您的语音或文字指令创建、编辑或查看日历行程。我们不会在服务器上存储您的完整日历历史。
  • 邮件数据 (Mailto)零 Gmail API
    我们不会申请读取或发送您 Gmail 邮件的权限。所有邮件草稿均通过 mailto: 链接在您的本地设备上生成，您的邮件内容始终保留在您自己的邮件客户端中。
  • 语音数据
    语音输入仅用于实时解析意图。我们不会存储、出售或将您的语音数据用于广告目的。
  • 身份验证令牌
    Google OAuth 令牌仅存储在您浏览器的本地存储中，不会发送至我们的服务器，退出登录时自动清除。

3. 数据存储与安全

  • 最小化存储：我们不会在服务器上存储您的日历行程、邮件内容或语音录音。
  • 加密基础设施：必要的身份验证信息均存储在加密的安全服务器上。
  • 严禁第三方共享：除提供服务所必需（如 Google API、Anthropic Claude API、OpenAI Whisper API）外，我们不会将您的个人或项目数据共享给任何第三方。

4. 第三方服务

  • Google APIs — OAuth 授权、日历。受 Google 隐私政策约束。
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI 意图处理，不用于模型训练。受 Anthropic 隐私政策约束。
  • OpenAI Whisper API — 语音转文字，不长期存储。受 OpenAI 隐私政策约束。
  • Vercel — 托管基础设施。受 Vercel 隐私政策约束。

5. 您的权利

  • 您可随时通过 Google 安全设置撤销我们对您 Google 账号的访问权限。
  • 一旦撤销授权，我们将无法再访问您的任何数据。
  • 撤销授权后，我们不保留任何用户数据。

6. 政策更新
